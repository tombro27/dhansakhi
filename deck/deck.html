02 · Brief about the idea
Brief about the idea

A bank-grade AI co-pilot that turns scattered savings into a guided plan

India's mass-affluent customer has money in 5+ places and advice in none. DhanSakhi fixes both.

The one-line pitch

DhanSakhi gives every IDBI customer a personal robo-advisor: one-tap RBI Account Aggregator consent pulls all their holdings, AI builds goal-based plans, every recommendation carries a plain-language “why”, and a vernacular conversational advisor (Gemini) answers anything — then maps goals to real IDBI + LIC products.

AA-native aggregation Explainable AI Vernacular advice IDBI + LIC cross-sell

Why it matters now

  • Trusted rails are ready: RBI AA covers 2.61B accounts / 252.9M users; NSDL & CDSL are live FIPs (Dec 2025).
  • IDBI already owns the shelf: MF distributor (ARN 0058), broking (IDBI Capital), NPS POP — and LIC (~49% owner) for insurance.
  • Advice is a luxury: quality advisory is HNI-only; the mass-affluent get DIY apps or mis-selling.
  • AI mandate + inclusion: explainable, multilingual advice is both a regulatory tailwind and a PSU-bank mission.

03 · Opportunities
Opportunities

Different, defensible, and built to solve the real problem

How is it different?

  • AA-native, not CAS/PDF imports (the friction INDmoney & HDFC SmartWealth still carry).
  • Explainable AI rationale vs black-box curation (Scripbox) or rule-based recs (everyone).
  • Vernacular advisor in 6 languages — almost no competitor offers this at depth.

How does it solve it?

  • One 360° view across bank, MF, demat, EPF, NPS & insurance.
  • AI risk-profile → target allocation → rebalancing with reasons.
  • Goal plans funded by the right IDBI/LIC product, not a push-sale.

USP

“Trust of a bank + intelligence of a super-app.” The intersection no one owns:

  • AA-native aggregation
  • Explainable, SEBI-aware advice
  • Bank + LIC goal-completion

Market opportunity: India's fastest-growing, least-served mass-affluent cohort (Tier-2/3 AUM up ~13% over six years), reachable through IDBI's existing customer base and AA rails — with a clear path from prototype to the bank's sandbox/POC.

04 · Features
List of features

What the solution offers

  • KYC-lite onboarding — DigiLocker/Aadhaar-style, paperless.
  • RBI AA consent artefact — purpose, scope, expiry, one-tap revoke.
  • 360° net-worth dashboard — bank + FD + MF + demat + EPF + NPS + LIC.
  • AI risk profiling — quiz → Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive, explained.
  • Goal-based planning — retirement, education, home, emergency; SIP-to-goal math.
  • Explainable portfolio advice — target vs current + “why” per rebalance.
  • Vernacular conversational advisor — Gemini, 6 languages, suitability-aware.
  • IDBI + LIC cross-sell engine — next-best-product mapped to goal gaps.
  • Trust & compliance layer — SEBI guardrails, DPDP consent, no-PII badge.
  • Cross-sell ROI model — benchmark-anchored revenue projection for the bank.

★ Demo “wow” features: the AA consent artefact, the explainable-AI rationale, and the vernacular advisor.
