# DhanSakhi — 3-Minute Demo Video Script & Shot List

> **Goal:** problem-first hook, then one flawless end-to-end "golden path" demo.
> Target length **3:00**. Narration is ~150 words/min — this script is paced to fit.
> **Record a backup screen-capture** of the full run — live fintech demos break, and a failed
> live demo is a top score-killer.

## Before you hit record (setup)
1. Run the deployed app (or `npm run dev`). For the richest demo, set **`ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`** so the
   advisor + explainability are live Claude (real vernacular). Without a key it still works (curated fallback).
2. In the top bar, **persona = Meena Iyer**, **language = English** to start.
3. Open in a clean full-screen browser window (hide bookmarks/extensions). 1440×900 looks best.
4. Start from the **landing page** (`/`).

## Timed script

### 0:00 – 0:25 · The hook (problem)
**On screen:** Landing page hero + the "problem" card (Meena).
**Say:**
> "Meet Meena — 38, a Pune-based IDBI customer. Her money is scattered across five institutions:
> bank FDs, mutual fund folios, demat, EPF, an old LIC policy. She has **no single view, and no
> affordable advice** — because in India, real wealth advice is reserved for the rich. Millions of
> IDBI's customers are exactly like her. That's the problem **DhanSakhi** solves."

### 0:25 – 0:40 · The solution in one line
**On screen:** Click **"Launch live demo"** → onboarding step 1 (Identity).
**Say:**
> "DhanSakhi is an AI robo-advisor inside IDBI's trust — it aggregates everything, plans her goals,
> explains every recommendation, and talks to her in her own language. Watch the whole journey."

### 0:40 – 1:08 · The 'wow' — Account Aggregator (the differentiator)
**On screen:** Click **Continue** → the **RBI Account Aggregator consent** screen. Point to the
consent details, then click **Approve consent** and let the accounts link in.
**Say:**
> "This is the magic. One **RBI Account Aggregator** consent — data-blind, revocable, DPDP-compliant.
> No PDF uploads, no screen-scraping. In seconds, DhanSakhi pulls **all** her held-away accounts —
> bank, mutual funds, demat, EPF, insurance — the way competitors still can't."

### 1:08 – 1:28 · Risk profile → 360° dashboard
**On screen:** Risk quiz appears → it's pre-answered → click **See my plan** → **Go to dashboard**.
Hover the allocation donut and the "current vs target" bars.
**Say:**
> "An AI risk profile sets her target allocation. And here's her **complete financial life on one
> screen** — ₹39 lakh net worth across eight accounts she'd never seen together — with exactly where
> she's off-target."

### 1:28 – 1:52 · Explainable AI (the trust feature)
**On screen:** Click **AI Advice**. Let the "Why this plan suits you" narrative render; scroll the
rebalancing cards showing the per-action "why".
**Say:**
> "Every recommendation comes with a plain-language **'why'** — generated by Claude, tied to her
> profile and goals. No black box. This is what makes AI advice **trustworthy** — and it's exactly
> what SEBI's transparency rules expect."

### 1:52 – 2:20 · Vernacular advisor (inclusion)
**On screen:** Top bar → switch language to **हिंदी (Hindi)**. Open **Advisor**. Click a suggestion
like *"How do I reach my retirement goal?"* and let the reply come in.
**Say:**
> "And she can just… ask. In Hindi, Marathi, Tamil — six languages. 'How do I reach my retirement
> goal?' DhanSakhi answers with her real numbers — a financial advisor in her pocket, in her language.
> That's wealth advice for the next 500 million Indians."

### 2:20 – 2:42 · Cross-sell + ROI (why IDBI wins)
**On screen:** Click **For You** (cross-sell). Show the LIC term + NPS-via-IDBI offers, then the ROI band.
**Say:**
> "Goals map to **real IDBI and LIC products** — a LIC term plan, NPS through IDBI's own licence —
> framed as completing her goal, not a sales push. For the bank, that's a **6× cross-sell lift** and
> a clear revenue case — a moat no standalone app can copy."

### 2:42 – 3:00 · Compliance + close
**On screen:** Click **Trust & Safety** briefly, then cut to the landing/title.
**Say:**
> "All of it is regulation-aware by design — RBI Account Aggregator, SEBI guardrails, DPDP, synthetic
> data. **DhanSakhi: the trust of a bank, the intelligence of a super-app.** Ready for IDBI's sandbox.
> Thank you."

## Tips
- Speak to the **value**, not the UI ("watch the whole journey," not "now I click this button").
- Keep the cursor calm; pause ~1s on each hero moment (consent, the "why" card, the Hindi reply).
- If recording live Claude, do one rehearsal so latency doesn't surprise you; otherwise the fallback
  is instant and looks identical.
- End a few seconds early — never run over 3:00.
